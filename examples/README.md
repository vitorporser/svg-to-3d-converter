# SVG to 3D Conversion Examples

This folder showcases the transformation capabilities of **[svg3d.app](https://svg3d.app)**, focusing on geometry accuracy and high-end material rendering.

## 🔄 The Transformation: 2D to 3D

The primary goal of the tool is to take standard vector paths and apply depth, lighting, and beveling. Below is a real-world example of a 2D icon transformed into a polished 3D asset.

### Before and After
![SVG to 3D Transformation](../svg-to-3d-transformation-example.webp)

### What happened here?
1.  **Path Extrusion:** The flat SVG coordinates were converted into a 3D mesh.
2.  **Edge Softening:** We applied a "Soft Edges" (bevel) effect to catch highlights, creating a premium "puffy" look.
3.  **Material Application:** A high-reflection blue material was applied to demonstrate depth.

---

## 💡 Best Practices for Best Results
To get the most out of **svg3d.app**, we recommend:
* **Clean Paths:** Use SVGs with simplified paths for faster browser rendering.
* **Closed Shapes:** Ensure your vector shapes are closed for perfect 3D solid generation.
* **View Contrast:** Use the "Switch to light/dark background" toggle in the app to check your material reflections.
